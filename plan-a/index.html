PLAN A
2025
PLAN A is a lyric video created for recording artist Ski Team’s single “Plan A.” The project focused on building a typographic visual language that could carry the tone, rhythm, and emotional atmosphere of the track across motion.
Design
The visual system centers typography as both image and pacing device. Through scale, movement, repetition, and contrast, the video translates the song’s energy into a motion-driven experience. Graphic assets and type treatments were developed to support the track’s shifting intensity while maintaining a cohesive visual language.
Outcome
  • A completed lyric video for Ski Team’s single “Plan A”
  • A motion system built through typographic animation and graphic assets
  • A visual direction that supports the single’s release across digital platforms
Context
Role Design Assist
Duration 2 weeks
Skills After Effects, Illustrator
