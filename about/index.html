Hi!

My name is Katelyn.

I’m a multidisciplinary designer who tends to fall down internet rabbit holes.

I'm interested in how people organize information, how identities become systems, and why the things we ignore every day often make the most interesting design material. My work spans branding, editorial, motion, and digital experiences, but it's usually held together by research, storytelling, and probably too many browser tabs.

I've worked with global agencies, boutique studios, nonprofits, and independent artists in Los Angeles, New York, and Seoul.

Outside of design, I'm probably collecting images on Are.na, cooking for friends, or slowly working my way through the stack of books next to my bed.
