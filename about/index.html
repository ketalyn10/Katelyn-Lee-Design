Hi!

My name is Katelyn.

I’m a designer who finds the most excitement in working with stories — not just telling them, but building the systems that hold them.

A lot of my work starts with things that already exist, whether that be in browser history, found images, or small patterns in behavior. I'm interested in how narratives emerge from structure, and how systems quietly shape what we understand.

Outside of design work, I’m usually trying new recipes, slowly working my way through my reading list, or making ikebana.
